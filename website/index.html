InSight

Watch the Video

InSight is the first product of Vigilant Technologies which is designed for meeting the increasing online surveillance demand of the customers by implementing state-of-the-art cloud, image processing and mobile technologies. In the standby position, The product actively, with the help of image processing and sensors, watches living area of the user home and notifies user upon detecting any motion, excessive temperature or sound occasion inside the area also provides images and videos of the living area with the notifications for providing required insights to the user about the abnormal event. InSight also provides on- demand connection to their devices over the Internet. InSight also able to categorize notifications which will minimize the user distraction with false alarms.
