Vigilant Technologies is a smart security systems startup. We aim to simplify home security systems, and making them as effective and convenient as they get. To do so, we develop and install state of art techniques in artificial intelligence, image processing, cloud computing systems, and electronics.
We have started developing our first product, inSight, in 2014, in which we have progressed dramatically since then, and we are ready to publicise our first prototype. inSight is a smart personal surveillance system that is designed to address the gap in the home security market arising from the absence of a product that provides efficient and convenient monitoring affordably.

Simplifying Security

InSight

Watch the Video

inSight is a unique smart security system designed to provide you monitoring and security of your home. It is installed with 1080p HD camera to capture images or record video of an important event at your home. inSight detects any motion in vision, analyses it via image processing techniques to check whether there is an actual event (such as a person while you are not at home), and gives informative notifications to you on your mobile.

We have designed inSight to be easy to use and effective in providing your home security. Here are some of inSight’s unique qualities:

Easy Instalment: Just plug it in and it starts running

inSight requires little effort to get running! Just take it out of the box, and plug it in. No other cables, or professional help to install inSight in your house. Start monitoring your house in seconds!

Setup Under a Minute

You can connect inSight to your home’s WiFi and match it with your mobile phone using our QR-code interface in under a minute. Using our mobile application, just scan the QR code in inSight’s box, type in your WiFi name and password, let inSight take a picture of the QR code appearing in your mobile phone’s screen. It is that easy!

Smart Alerts

inSight does not watch your house passively, it analyses its environment. inSight comes with a novel image processing and artificial intelligence algorithm to distinguish humans (as possible intruders) from other moving objects, such as your pet! This way, inSight decreases false alarm rates, and notifies you only if there is an event worthy of your attention.

Watch Mode

In case of a person is detected in your home while it is set on Watch-mode, inSight captures a close shot portrait of intruder in addition to regular notification media, so that you see who is at your home in HD quality, even from distance!

Data Safety: Your private data is safe with us

Your home is your most private place, and we value its privacy as much as you do. Thus we took both hardware and software measures to assure the safety of your data. All data processing, including our novel image processing algorithms, is run on your inSight. Your data leaves inSight securely encrypted, travels through cloud that way, only to be decrypted in your mobile. No one can watch your house over inSight, not even us!
